About us

Our Story

Located on the southern tip of Phu Quoc Island, La Vista is a hidden tropical retreat.

Relax in one of our spacious 215 villas, each with a private swimming pool. Indulge in our hill-top Plumeria Spa and Fitness Centre. Diverse culinary options range from Vietnamese delights at The Market and fusion cuisine at Corallo Restaurant’ Plumeria Spa and Fitness Centre.

Our Mission

La Vista commits to provide our corporate and leisure traveller a
consistent warm & friendly guest experience & fond memories
through our nurtured staff who experience joy and pride
at work while adhering to our social responsibilities.

Our Vision

La Vista Phu Quoc aspires to be the most preferred 5 - star
‘Environment-sensitive’ villa, through innovative products and
services, following best practices in the industry to
achieve sustainable growth.
