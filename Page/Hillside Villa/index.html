Hillside Villa

Rises above the rest

A modern and elegant home-away-from-home for families and friends, these 400sqm villas cover three floors. They include a private infinity pool, master king-sized ensuite bedroom. Positioned hilltop, the Hillside Villas enjoy a partial view of the ocean as well as optimal privacy.

This luxury villa is equipped with Wi-Fi and an Apple TV. A Bose sound system runs throughout the villa and includes speakers in every bedroom. While all rooms are designed to take advantage of the delightful tropical breezes, the doors can be closed if you desire protection from the elements.
